# Aici Proof Video Design

## Style Prompt

Quiet, technical product proof for a local-first developer tool. The video should feel like a serious engineering demo, not a launch trailer: paper-like canvas, crisp terminal/report screenshots, compact labels, restrained motion, and clear evidence that the product fails pull requests before broken AI behavior ships.

## Colors

- Background: `#f8f5ee`
- Ink: `#202632`
- Muted ink: `#667085`
- Line: `#ddd7cb`
- Pass accent: `#158455`
- Fail accent: `#c2412d`
- Terminal background: `#202632`

## Typography

- Primary: Inter, system sans-serif
- Monospace: JetBrains Mono, ui-monospace
- Large headings use tight but readable weights; proof labels stay small and scannable.

## What NOT to Do

- No generic blue-purple AI gradients.
- No stock imagery or fake dashboards.
- No talking-head layout.
- No exaggerated claims like "prevents all leaks."
- No decorative card grids that obscure the screenshots.
